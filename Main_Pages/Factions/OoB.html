The Order of Blades

The Order of Blades is a group of warforged that were abandoned in the Wasteland after the Civil War ended. Outraged a group of specialized warforged gathered the surviors and formed the order of blades to exact revenge on the kingdom that abandoned them. Their time in the Wasteland was spent arming themselves for war with the left overs, inculding fixing up and reactiving a colossal warforged. Their attack on the captial however was stopped by the party when the party went in and defeated the Leadears of the Order of Blades.


Known Important Members

0000 - Izeah

0000 or Izeah was the true Lord of Blades and the one who began the revoult. He is also Issac's son.


0001 - Blade

0001 or Blade was Izeah's second in command and was the Lord of Blades to the public to keep Izeah's existance secret.


0002 - Bastion

0002 or Bastion was one of the first attempts at creating a colossal warfoged. While not a success in that regard he was still a formidable foe.


0003 - Ace

0003 or Ace was the first of the sniper class of warforged. While many attempts were made none were ever as successful as her.


0004 - Immortal

0004 or Immortal was one of the artificers orignally in charge of making warforged. When her life neared its end she expiremented on herself turning her into something else.


0005 - Sage

0005 or Sage was once a powerful mage before falling in the civil war. Using his magic he was keep his soul alive long enough to be transfered to a Warforged body.


0006 - Nameless

0006 or Nameless was an assassin and scout during the civil war. Wanting to improve their prowess they voulnteered to become a warforged.
