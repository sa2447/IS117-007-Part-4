The Royals

The Royals are a group of 4 overgods from another dimension. When the strongest of them the Emperor decided he wanted to rule alone his attempt at Domination left their world broken. As it would soon be destroyed the Royals took their children and their followers with them in serch of a new place to settle. They ended up landing on this contenint and were sealed for a very long time. They were eventually freed by the party and mangaed to end the conflict between their children and now plan in case the Emperor returns.
