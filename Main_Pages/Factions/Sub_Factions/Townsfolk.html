Known Important Members

Dave

Dave was once a normal town guard before being rescued by the party. through traveling with them he was become quite the adept fighter and despite once being fully human due to circumstances outside his control he is now a strange mix of human, dragon, and royal magic. He is also the town's Secretarian.


Aseir

Aseir is a gold dragon and one of Artix's children. He is a shapeshifter and for time decided to start a trading company called G.I.G while pretening to be a Goblin. He is the town's Loremonger.


G0bL1N

G0bL1N is an artifical goblin created by Rell. He is able to absorb the traits of creatures he consumes and mix them to gain the best traits of each creature. He is the town's problem.
