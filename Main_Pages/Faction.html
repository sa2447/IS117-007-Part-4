Factions


There are many Prominent Factions On the continent each with their own members and goals not all are benevolent.

SilverKeep

A group founded by the players of the campagin, consists of players and the npcs in their town.

Silverkeep
Councel of Dragons

The direct counsel of Dragons is a group of ancient dragons who are the direct decendents of their ancestor "Queen", nowadays the group inculdes other dragonkin and their chosen heros.

Councel of Giants

The direct counsel of Giants is a group of ancient giants who are the direct decendents of their ancestor "King", nowadays the group inculdes other giantkin and their chosen heros.

Human Kingdoms

A group formed at the end of the Civil war, someties called the Mortal Kingdoms as all types of humanoids reside within its borders. Run by King Issac Falconreach the 1st

A group founded the remaining warforged headed by the Lord of Blades, together they plan for revenge on those that abandoned them.
