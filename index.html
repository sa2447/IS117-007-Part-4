Overview

  Dragons and Giants is a Dungeons and Dragons Campagin run by me for several of my friends. This site serves as a way to keep track of the events of the campagin as well as let others see what has been going on. Throughout this site you will see various bits of important information about the campagin. World lore will tell you about the campagin before the players were involved in the story. Campagin Lore is the events that have happened since the players entered the story. Factions will let you know about the various factions in the campagin. Finally Map will show you the overall map for the campagin and a brief description of the important locations. This site is also written, with only information that the players currently have and know to avoid spoilers, as such some important characters are either refered to by their title or unkown since the players do not have that informations.
