The Counsel of Dragons

The Counsel of Dragons is made up of six main Ancient Dragons as well as the dragonkin and others under them. The Main Six of the counsel are originally from the same plane of existance as the Royals. They are the direct children on the Royal known as "Queen", as such they have extraordinary power that rivals if not surpasses many of the gods. Each one of the six is also the embodyment of a certain aspect, the aspects being "Power", "Magic", "Fire", "Alchemy", "Creativity", "Decay". These dragons have chosen the main party to be their heroes and to help them end the conflict between them.


Known Important Members

Artix

Artix is the oldest of Ancient dragons and is the aspect of Power. He is a very strict and by the book dragon believing it is his duty and his duty alone to protect his siblings. In combat he is the strongest dragon, despite having no intrest in magic his raw power and Fire breath are more than enough. He often butts heads with Raynet as Raynet takes things to casually. While he did not choose any of the main party to bless he did bless Dave as Dave and Artix's son had become friends.


Raynet

Raynet is the second oldest of Ancient dragons and is the aspect of Magic. He is the exact opposite of his brother Artix, he is more easy going and finds rules boring. When it comes to magic Raynet is unmatched which more than makes up for his lack of normal combat skills. As the former conduit of magic in the dimension the Royals are from Raynet is not bound by the rules and restrictions that exist here and abuses his magic for petty things like making clones so he can avoid meetings. Despite his carefree attitude Raynet is the only one of the Ancient Dragons to beat Artix in a fight. He choose to bless Thrice of the main party.


Cedris

Cedris is one of three of the ancient dragons that were hatched at the same time. He is the aspect of fire, and takes after Artix the most. He however like the others that hatche with him decided to mix both physcial combat and magic making him a great all-around fighter. He choose to bless Klink of the main party, also blessing her Axebeak turning it into a Red Dragon for Klink to ride into combat.


Sheeza

Sheeza another one of the three of the ancient dragons that were hatched at the same time. She is the aspect of Creativity, and mostly keeps to herself prefering to make new things. She however like the others that hatched with her decided to mix both physcial combat and magic making her a great all-around fighter, additonally she decided to add her various creations into her fighting style. She choose to bless Tifatina of the main party as they have a shared joy in creating things.


Malefact

Malefact is one of three of the ancient dragons that were hatched at the same time. He is the aspect of Alchemy, and shares many traits with his sister Sheeza. He however focused more on the creation of new alchemical componets and potions and like the others that hatche with him decided to mix both physcial combat and magic making him a great all-around fighter, while also using his concotions to heal and buff his allies. He choose to bless Shalith of the main party as her slime like nature made her a perfect match for his potions. combat.


Zorbak

Zorbak is the youngest of the ancient dragons. He is the aspect of Decay, he spent much of his time in solutude as he learned to control his aspect. Once he did however he started to take more after Raynet as he believes his aspect is the strongest and leaves him with no weakness now that its undercontrol. Unlike his siblings the extra time he spent learning to control his aspect meant he relies on it in fights and adpated his fighting style accordingly. He choose to bless Bakar of the main party.
