Known Important Members

Bakar

Bakar is an 18th level Leonin Oath of Conquest Paladin. He is one of the main damage dealers of the party and is currently in a relationship with Klink's mom. He has also reached Demi-god status and is the town's Documancer.


Klink

Klink is an 18th level Goblin Cleric of Life. She is the main healer of the party and is resistant to almost everything but has awful luck against Blights. She has also reached Demi-god status and is the town's Obviator.


Shalith

Shalith is an 18th level tiefling zelot Barbarian. She is the main tank of the group and is also part ooze and has a nalfeshnee sealed inside her. She has also reached Demi-god status and is the town's Hoardsperson.


Thrice

Thrice is an 18th level goliath champion fighter. He is the party's secondary damage dealer. Also he is part of the dragon faction despite technically being a giantkin. He has also reached Demi-god status and is the town's Occultant.


Tifatina

Tifatina is an 18th level gnome Artillirst artificer. She is the party's flex role and can support or damgage when needed. She has created several magic items and her heart has even been replaced by one replacing her blood with oil. She has also reached demi-god status and is the party's Cartographer.
