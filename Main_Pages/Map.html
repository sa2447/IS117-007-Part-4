Important Locations

Falconreach- The capital of the continent and where many of the humanoid races came to reside when the Civil War ended. A Large Steam punk city seperated into rings, where each ring is a specfic district.


Throne of Blades- Where the faction of rogue warforged resided, home to the Order of Blades. Mostly made up of abadoned factories and military bases out in the Wasteland.


Silverkeep- Where the main party of player characters settled and govern over as their main base. Silverkeep is currently a medium village


Giant's Throne- Where the Counsel of Giants reside after returning to the land. It is a castle in the clouds held up by strange magic.


Dragon's Perch- Where the chosen of the Counsel of Dragons settled, It is a large mountinous area carried on the back of a gargantuan Dragon Turtle.


Throne of Blades- Where the faction of rogue warforged resided, home to the Order of Blades.


Stone Garrison- Where the chosen of the Counsel of Giants settled, it is a small village.


Bekensdale- Where the main party first met, it was a small village before it was attacked and destroyed.
